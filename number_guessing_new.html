Number Guessing Game

How to Play ?

  • To set the minimum number and maximum number that you want to predict, add a lower bound and an upper bound to the below boxes.
  • Next, enter the guesstimate number and press enter key.
  • Depend on the numbers you added your number of attempts will change: Play carefully; we'll give you tips after each attempt.
  • After add numbers decide what you want to pay : Easy or Hard.
  • If you like to see the numbers you guessed please press my guesses button after you done.
